# Introduction

David J. C. MacKay (1967–2016) combined clarity of thought with kindness and curiosity. He had a rare talent for turning messy questions into crisp, checkable reasoning - often with nothing more than a back-of-the-envelope calculation and an insistence on *numbers, not adjectives*.

This booklet gathers short recollections from people who knew David either peronally or through his works in different contexts: as a teacher, colleague, collaborator, creator, mentor and friend. They are varied in style and length; together they aim to preserve something of his voice and influence.
