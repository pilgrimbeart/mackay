# David the person
As these tributes to David flowed in, a strong theme emerged. Why do so many people share the urge to bear witness to not just a great thinker, but an inspirational person? The overwhelming sense I have is one of what the Greeks called ἀγάπη - the selfless, unconditional love of universal compassion. David's goodwill to the universe is here reflected back. So to close, here are some particularly personal memories of his remarkable being.
