# Introduction

David J. C. MacKay (1967–2016) combined clarity of thought with kindness and curiosity, changing people's lives directly and indirectly across many different subjects. He had a rare talent for turning messy questions into crisp, checkable reasoning and an insistence on *numbers, not adjectives*, delivered with wit, warmth and compassion.

Here are recollections from people who knew David either personally or through his works: as teacher, colleague, collaborator, creator, mentor and friend. Varied in style and length, together they aim to preserve something of his voice and influence.

I've attempted the hopeless task of organising these by topic, but inevitably there's a lot of overlap because, as David was fond of saying, "everything is connected".

Pilgrim Beart
